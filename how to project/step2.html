Step 2

The next step is the most important: applying the peanut butter and jelly. It all depends on the ratio. I think an equal amount of each makes it the best. The flavor of jelly is also up to you although I like grape jelly the best.

  • Using a safe knife apply peanut butter to one piece of bread
  • Use the same knife to add your favorite jelly to the other piece of bread
